Weapons & Attachments

Marksman Rifles

High accuracy builds with low fire rate. Pair with long barrels, advanced scopes, angled grips.

Close Quarters

Shotguns and SMGs thrive with suppressors, recoil pads, and quickdraw mags.

Improvised Weapons

Crafted sticks and blades scale with melee skills. Use them for silent takedowns when ammo is scarce.

Maps & Extractions

Sunken Reeds

Low visibility marsh. Use thermal sights and mark mudslides that slow sprint speed.

Neon Garrison

Urban rooftops with zipline shortcuts. Weather dictates drone detection range.

Polar Gravel

Arctic quarry. Protect stamina with heat packs; extraction lifts close during blizzards.

Ember Coop

Industrial zone full of turret nests. Carry EMP seedpods.

Skygrain Port

Freighter maze with crane-based extractions. Rain increases footstep noise.
